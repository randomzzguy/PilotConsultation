Our Services

Comprehensive marine inspection services designed to ensure safety, compliance, and operational excellence

Commercial Surface Supplied Air Diving System Inspection

Our team of experienced and certified technicians provides comprehensive inspection services in accordance with IMCA standards.

This includes an inspection and function test of the equipment to ensure compliance with safety standards. Our qualified team can also assist vessel owners in obtaining class diving notation.

IMCA Certified

Marine Vessel Inspections

We conduct thorough assessments of marine vessels to evaluate their condition and suitability for operation. Our inspections cover various aspects of the vessel, including:

Supervision

We supervise vessels during building, rebuild, and major repair activities.

Pre-Hire Inspection

We assess the condition of the vessel before it is hired to ensure it meets the necessary standards.

General Condition & Bunker Survey

Comprehensive vessel condition checks and bunker fuel quality/quantity inspections.

eCMID Inspection

We provide eCMID inspection up to the IMCA marine division requirements.
